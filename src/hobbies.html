Motorsports

Currently, I mainly watch the Formula One world championship but I love all forms of motorsport. My favorite driver of all time is Sebastian Vettel, and my favorite team is Ferrari. I have always loved motorsports and cars, but my love for motorsports really took off during the pandemic. I am also an avid simracer, racing on games such as the Formula One games, Assetto Corsa, RFactor 2, and Forza Horizon 5. My dream car is either the BMW E60 M5 or the Ferrari F40.

Counter Strike

Counter Strike is a video game series created by Valve. I have been playing it since 2018, starting from Counter Strike: Global Offensive to Counter Strike 2. I enjoy the competitive gameplay and the teamwork involved in the game. I am also a big fan of Counter Strike Esports. I have been a Natus Vincere fan since 2020 but I enjoy watching all teams. While I play a lot less now, I always enjoy keeping up with the game and playing an occasional match with friends.

Video Games

Other than Counter Strike and Racing games, I enjoy many other games. Recently, I finally finished Elden Ring, enjoying it much more than I thought I would. I also really enjoyed the Metro Series of games, portraying a grim post nuclear world. I am currently finishing Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, Chrono Trigger, and the original Half Life. I also enjoy visual novels, with my favorite being Once Again. I really enjoyed the camera mechanics and the light hearted story. If you have any recommendations for games, please let me know!

PC Building

I created my first custom PC in 7th grade. It has an fx8350 and an rx240. Ever since then, I have been fascinated by computer hardware and building PCs. Every year, I would research the best upgrade for my PC and get it for my birthday. Right now I have a couple of different computers. My daily computer has a Ryzen 5 3600 and an RX 6700 XT, with 32 GBs of ram. At college, I have another PC, with an Xeon E5-2680 v4 and an RX 480. I also use a Surface Pro 6 with Ubuntu, an HP Envy 15 inch with Arch and Hyprland and a Lenovo Legion GO. I have a bunch of different peripherials so if you're curious about that please contact me!
